Our Approach

Intelligence without exposure.

In traditional ML systems, all data must travel to a central server. NEXUS inverts this — the model travels to the data. Each client trains on its own private market history and sends only weight updates back.

The result is a global model that has learned from AAPL, TSLA, MSFT, AMZN and GOOGL data — without any of those datasets ever leaving their respective nodes.
